11 August 2021

EvilJackCarver updated:

  • Added a variable to projectiles to determine if it will explode welderfuel tanks or Benzin roaches.
  • Benzin roaches no longer explode from blunt-force trauma.
  • Benzin roaches now leak welder fuel when killed.
  • Benzin roaches now gib when exploded. You can now detonate Benzin roaches after their death if you so desire. (Why anyone would WANT to do that, is beyond me.)
  • Buffed Benzin health up to 25 to counteract the above.
  • Welderfuel tanks no longer explode if shot at by bullets that shouldn't explode them; they will instead leak.

07 August 2021

Sly9sky updated:

  • Autolathes will no longer print magazines that are less than full
  • Autolathes can have their print quality restored with very-high-quality parts

06 August 2021

EvilJackCarver updated:

  • Adds trichloramine, a flammable gas that's also an oxidiser.
  • Adds liquid trichloramine, a reagent that's dangerous to make.
  • Adds monochloramine, which essentially the same as trichloramine but not flammable (intended for planet gas use)
  • Adds ausgiftrol, a powerful antivenin that's 4 times as effective at purging roach and spider venom compared to dylovene, but is only 75% as effective of an antitoxin.

  • Slightly increased stock of Medbay Lobby's MicroMed vendor until an option is added to manually refill the vendor.
  • Moved Prison Cell security cameras to the hallway to provide cleaner coverage.

  • Added showers to Aegis and Engineering dorms.
  • Corrected various mislabeled doors, especially the Heads of Staff relates ones.
  • Fixed the research shuttle tank storage unit not spawning with phoron tanks for fuel.
  • Fixed trade dock cameras using the medbay camera network instead of third section one.
  • Added missing hull tiles above shooting range so the room now actually has a ceiling.
