24 September 2022

EvilJackCarver updated:

  • Adds a warning if the number of mobs gets too insane.

21 September 2022

EvilJackCarver updated:

  • All firearm names now have the \improper flag, turning their examine text from "This is Wintermute" to "This is a Wintermute".
  • Minor spelling and grammar tweaks to firearms and firearm mods.
  • Maverick now has a diskette.
  • Rebalanced some firearms with low damage.
  • Blob is now a bit tougher to beat.
  • Shield generator room is now listed as a critical area for purposes of gridchecks.
  • Shield generator air alarm changed to a different subtype, to hopefully fix an issue where the vents would cut off.
  • Toxins' mixing chambers now use fireproof glass.
  • Engineering and Medical equipment storage now have the correct rolls of cordon tape.
  • Added missing section of hull plating near the Cargo shuttle.
  • Replaced .40 magnum rubber ammo in the armoury with .20 rifle rubber ammo.
  • Added OC spray refill stations around Security.
  • The Church can now print cruciforms again.
  • Night shift has finally gotten around to installing some of the signs on the ship. You can examine them to see what they mean.
  • Added a backup emergency SMES unit. Its substation is just forward of Atmospherics.
  • Medbay now has a storage area in the elevated walkway between medical and R&D.
  • Medbay now has sinks.
  • Virology now has a centrifuge and a pathogenic isolator.
  • Night shift's electricians have gotten lazy again. Same for the janitors.
  • All substations now have at least one guaranteed power cell spawn, with a chance at more.

  • Heavily remodeled the centcom map.
  • Changed the holodeck maps to align with the holodeck doors

02 August 2022

EvilJackCarver updated:

  • Hugs will now give sanity if the person doing the hugging or the person being hugged has low enough sanity.
  • Benzins can now continue to leak while being dragged, or when hit with a sharp object.
  • Added a config option to allow the server operator to change the number of exoplanets that are generated.
  • Ports EclipseStation13 species-specific chemistry reaction framework.
  • Dispatcher can now announce the presence, and defeat, of a hivemind.
  • Adds basic framework for simplemob bonus damage.
  • Adds the Chiropteran Arms Maverick, a lever-action energy gun designed to bridge the gap between single-shot high-damage (e.g. the Paladin) and repeating energy weapons (e.g. pretty much every other laser out there sans the Squire).
  • Rejuvinating agent is a bit more difficult to make.
  • Minor under-the-hood changes for the Liquid Trichloramine reagent.
  • Tweaks to various wording in Eclipse configs.
  • Gas masks now filter out chloramines.
  • Fixes an issue where dummymobs created by the ghost icon generation system could be emailed.
